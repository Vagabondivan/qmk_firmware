# Commodore 64 keymap for Preonic Rev. 3

- QWERTY + GAMING (Retropie and MAME)

- Numbers and Symbols + Accented Letters + email address, signature and disclaimer (customize as you like).

- RGB Matrix underglow. 

- ADJUST layer (press LOWER and RAISE together) to switch between first 2 layers and to put the pcb in FLASH mode.

- TAP DANCE to switch on/off CAPSLOCK led (double tap left shift)

- TAP DANCE for Opening/Closing parenthesis/brackets: 1 TAP Parenthesis, 2 TAPs Square Brackets, 3 TAPs Curly Brackets
